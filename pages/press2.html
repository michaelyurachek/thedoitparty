Press Release:

We will be discussing America’s involvement in the Ukraine and Russia war and our opinion on it . To date, we have provided more than $64 billion in military assistance since Russia launched its premeditated, unprovoked, and brutal full-scale invasion of Ukraine on February 22, 2022. Approximately $67 billion in military assistance since Russia’s initial invasion of Ukraine in 2014.

When we are elected we will slowly stop all financial support to Ukraine and stop all military aid. Our opinion on this is that the U.S. needs to stop fighting other countries' wars and focus on rebuilding their country and economy for the US citizens. Although, we would like to plan a meeting between the two presidents to possibly discuss a peace deal over this pointless war.

If we cannot get Ukraine and Russia to agree to stop this war then the US will completely stop sending aid over to Ukraine. The US needs to focus on our own country instead of fighting for other countries. If you agree please vote now.
